Hasher is service to encrypt and save your files. You don't need any registration of account. Just load the files, enter your key and press the button.

If you don't need account, how app knows that your files is your?

Every file has its user identifier based on your encrypted IP address, it's unique for you and can't be repeated, and that is why you see only your files without registration.

Can developers see your files?

No, all files are encrypted by our algorithm and they can be decrypted only by key that you have. Keys are stored nowhere and only you know it.

If you want, you may to not save the files on the server, but just encrypt it and save on your computer. Next time you need it you can decrypt it back easily. You can do it on encrypt/decrypt page.
